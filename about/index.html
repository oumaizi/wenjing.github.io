About

Wenjing Liu is a graphic designer and creative coder who grew up in Guangdong, China and is currently based in London. Wenjing studied branding design at the Guangzhou Academy of Fine Arts before participating in the experimental communication programme at the Royal College of Art. His commerical work focus on brand expression through posters, moving images and coding. As a researcher, his work explores the socio-political effects of digital technology through installation, coding, posters and publications.

Wenjing Liu 是一名平面设计师和创意程序员，在中国广东长大，目前居住在伦敦。 他在参加英国皇家艺术学院的实验传播项目之前，曾在广州美术学院学习品牌设计。他的平面设计作品包含海报，品牌形象设计以及动态影像。作为一名研究员，他的作品通过装置、编码、海报和出版物数字技术的社会政治影响。
